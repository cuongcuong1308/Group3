TinyTots Company

Our mission

Over the years, at we have had one major goal; this goal is driven by the fact that we take your baby’s well-being as our central focus.

Our founding

Tiny Tots Baby Store founded in 2011 by Sulani Perks. The driving force for the establishment was being a mother to two children and understanding things first hand.

Our range is constantly expanding with more varieties and innovations. Every product in our baby store undergoes quality and durability testing leaves us confident that no matter what you purchase, you get a guarantee that you get the best.

Coupled with our excellent customer service, our baby shop is a delight to walk in to. We are hinged on the foundation of parent’s satisfaction, baby’s comfort, innovation and altogether it is what makes us unarguably and proudly the best place to shop in.

Early growth

Our baby store was initially an online based platform strictly in 2011.

From our strictly online based platform, we have moved to a boutique baby showroom in Bayswater, east of Melbourne.

Next Stage of our service....

With our expanding range and meeting the needs of our local community, we are took our store to a new Venue.

New store location No.1142 Burwood Hwy, Ferntree Gully, Victoria 3156.

Located at the base of beautiful Mount Dandenong, it is home to our innovative products, ranging from bed and bath items to feeding ,nursery , toys, and host of others. Our superb and very friendly customer service is positioned to deliver to you comfort.

How we meet your needs

We offer the best Price Beat Guarantee, extended 60 day return and exchange policy which go beyond standard ACCC policies.
